8. Click on any generated self-test entry (in any tab) - verify it opens the corresponding file in the editor.
9. Switch to the Tags tab, then reload the plugin (disable and re-enable in Settings > Community Plugins). Verify the Tags tab is still active after reload.
10. Verify the tab styling looks clean - active tab has an accent underline, inactive tabs are muted.
